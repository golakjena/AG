Atlanta Brain Injury Attorneys

The catastrophic and life-changing nature of a traumatic brain injury will affect the victim and their loved ones in countless ways. The pain of the initial trauma is soon overshadowed by the growing burden of the recovery process. The cost of treatment and recovery is overwhelming but not necessarily a stress the victim and their family have to tackle alone.

Have you or someone you know suffered a brain injury after a construction accident?

A report from the National Center for Injury Prevention and Control states that nearly 1.4 million people fall victim to traumatic brain injuries across the United States annually. What’s even more astonishing is that almost 50,000 of these incidents result in death. Our attorneys at Affleck and Gordon will fight to safeguard you or your loved one’s rights.

A 2004 Center for Disease Control report notes some of the leading causes of brain injuries include:

  • Construction accidents
  • Sports injuries
  • Falls
  • Motor vehicle accidents
  • Violent assaults
  • Occupational accidents

The brain injury victim’s main concern should be regaining his or her health and returning to a satisfactory quality of life. This is a lengthy, expensive and emotionally draining process. Proper legal assistance can alleviate these burdens. Please consider a consultation with our disability attorneys to discuss you or your loved one’s traumatic brain injury.
