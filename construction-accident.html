Atlanta Construction Accident Attorneys

Our Atlanta workers’ compensation attorneys can help individuals injured at construction sites. Although the industry as a whole strives towards safety, there are often jobsite accidents due to negligence. Considering the exposure to hazardous materials, heavy machinery, high elevation, explosives, etc., construction work is potentially lethal. As the construction industry continues to thrive across Atlanta, the number of these accidents will increase. Construction accident attorneys work to rebuild and rectify the wrongs done unto construction workers following their work-related accident.

Have you or a loved one been involved in a construction accident?

Although safety regulations exist, accidents still occur on the jobsite. The Center for Disease Control and Prevention notes that at least one percent of all construction workers require medical attention stemming from injuries annually.

Examples of circumstances in which individuals have been injured include:

  • Scaffolding accidents
  • Trauma to brain
  • Cave ins
  • Falls
  • Lifting injuries
  • Electrocution
  • Blunt force trauma
  • Burns
  • Chemical exposure
  • Severe lacerations, cuts, abrasions
  • Bone fractures and breaks

The Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 established an important benchmark in preserving workers’ rights in the American workforce. These rights extend to legally protect an injured worker. Our attorneys can help injured workers regain their financial footing and receive the benefits they deserve.
