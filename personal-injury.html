Atlanta Personal Injury Attorneys

Our experienced personal injury attorneys can provide detailed explanations of Georgia’s personal injury laws. These cover a variety of issues after someone has been injured by anothers’ carelessness, negligence or intentional wrongdoing. In some cases it can also cover property damage, such as your car in the event of an accident!

Have you or a loved one been injured in an accident?

With mounting medical bills and extended periods of time out of work due to injury, accident victims often find it difficult to pay their expenses. This leads to emotional distress for the individual and his or her family. Under personal injury law, accident victims are entitled to receive monetary compensation for their injuries.

Personal injury laws cover a vast range of events including:

  • scaffolding accidents
  • car accidents
  • slip and fall accidents
  • product liability
  • dog bites
  • medical malpractice
  • motorcycle accidents
  • truck accidents
  • nursing home malpractive
  • negligent security
